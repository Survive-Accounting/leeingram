# Integrations Overview

## Purpose

Survive Accounting relies on several external services to power different parts of the platform.

These integrations handle:

• course delivery  

• payments  

• teaching materials  

• team communication  

• time tracking

This document explains how each external system fits into the overall architecture.

---

# Core Integration Philosophy

The Survive Accounting system is designed so that:

• the internal pipeline manages content production  

• external tools handle delivery and operations

This separation allows the system to stay simple while leveraging specialized platforms.

---

# LearnWorlds

LearnWorlds is the primary platform used to deliver course content to students.

Students interact with Survive Accounting primarily through LearnWorlds courses.

---

## LearnWorlds Responsibilities

LearnWorlds manages:

• course hosting  

• student accounts  

• lesson navigation  

• quizzes and assessments  

• video playback  

• course progress tracking

---

## Integration Points

Teaching assets may link to LearnWorlds resources.

Examples:

lw_video_link  

lw_quiz_link  

These links allow internal teaching assets to connect to the final student experience.

---

# Stripe

Stripe handles payment processing.

Stripe is used to manage:

• course purchases  

• subscriptions  

• refunds  

• payment tracking

Stripe may also support future features such as:

• Greek organization licensing  

• campus subscriptions  

• bundled course access

---

# Google Sheets

Google Sheets serves as the **internal teaching whiteboard system**.

Each approved teaching asset generates a Google Sheet template.

These sheets are used for:

• tutoring sessions  

• instructional video recording  

• internal teaching preparation

Sheets contain structured tabs including:

Metadata  

Hidden Data  

Master Whiteboard  

Journal Entries  

Worked Steps  

Important Formulas  

Concepts  

Exam Traps

Sheets may also be embedded in LearnWorlds where appropriate.

---

# Google Drive

Google Drive stores all Google Sheet templates generated by the pipeline.

Drive provides:

• centralized storage  

• easy sharing  

• folder organization  

• access control

Teaching asset sheets should be organized in structured folders for easy management.

---

# Clockify

Clockify is used for time tracking by virtual assistants.

Clockify allows the founder to monitor:

• hours worked  

• task durations  

• production costs

This helps maintain efficient pipeline operations.

---

# Slack

Slack is the primary communication tool used by the team.

Slack supports:

• asynchronous communication  

• pipeline updates  

• quick coordination between VAs and the founder

Channels may be organized by pipeline stage or VA role.

---

# Future Integrations

Additional integrations may be added as the platform grows.

Possible future integrations include:

• analytics tools  

• automation services  

• CRM systems  

• campus licensing management tools

---

# Integration Design Principles

When adding integrations, the system should prioritize:

• minimal complexity  

• clear data boundaries  

• reliable automation  

• maintainability

External systems should remain loosely coupled whenever possible.
