# Greek Organization Licensing Portal — Roadmap

## Purpose

Survive Accounting plans to expand distribution through partnerships with university Greek organizations.

Fraternities and sororities often seek academic support resources for their members. A licensing model allows organizations to provide accounting exam preparation materials to their members.

This document outlines the long-term vision for a **self-service licensing portal** that allows Greek organizations to purchase and manage access to Survive Accounting courses.

---

# Why Greek Organizations

Greek organizations represent a strong distribution channel because:

• they contain large groups of students in the same classes  

• they often have academic support budgets  

• members benefit from shared study resources  

• organizations want to improve academic performance metrics

Licensing agreements can provide consistent revenue while helping students succeed.

---

# Licensing Model

The basic model is an **organization-level subscription**.

An organization purchases access to Survive Accounting for a defined group of members.

Examples:

• one semester license  

• annual license  

• course-specific access

Pricing may scale based on the number of members covered.

---

# Portal Goals

The licensing portal should allow organizations to:

• purchase licenses  

• manage member access  

• track usage  

• renew licenses

The goal is to create a **self-service system** that requires minimal manual administration.

---

# Organization Accounts

Each Greek organization would have its own account within the licensing portal.

Organization accounts may include:

• organization name  

• university  

• chapter information  

• administrator contact  

• active licenses

The organization administrator manages member access.

---

# Member Access

Members of a licensed organization should receive access to the relevant Survive Accounting courses.

Access could be granted through:

• invite links  

• organization login portals  

• access codes

LearnWorlds would manage the underlying student accounts.

---

# Stripe Integration

Stripe would handle payments for licensing.

Possible features include:

• organization subscription billing  

• invoice generation  

• license renewal payments

Stripe webhooks could update license status within the system.

---

# Course Access Mapping

Each license must map to specific courses.

Examples:

• Financial Accounting only  

• Managerial Accounting only  

• Full Survive Accounting bundle

The licensing system must connect organization licenses to LearnWorlds course access.

---

# Campus Routing

Organizations belong to specific universities.

The licensing system may eventually connect to the **Choose Your Campus** routing system.

This ensures students receive the correct course version for their school.

---

# Admin Dashboard

Organization administrators should have access to a simple dashboard.

Possible dashboard features:

• view active licenses  

• invite members  

• track member access  

• renew subscriptions

The dashboard should prioritize simplicity and clarity.

---

# Founder Admin Tools

The founder should be able to:

• view licensed organizations  

• monitor license activity  

• manually adjust access if needed  

• track revenue from organizational sales

These tools may live inside the internal Lovable dashboard.

---

# Long-Term Potential

If successful, Greek licensing could become a major growth channel.

Benefits include:

• predictable revenue streams  

• rapid campus adoption  

• strong word-of-mouth distribution

The licensing system should be designed to support expansion across many universities.
